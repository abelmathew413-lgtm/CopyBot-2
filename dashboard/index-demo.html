Wallet Watch

Under-the-radar Polymarket wallets ranked by a skill-vs-luck composite score, for manual review — not autopilot.

Composite score blends CLV, Sharpe ratio, lifetime & 90-day ROI, max drawdown, sample size (discounted for low market/capital diversity), and win rate into a single weighted ranking. When a component is unavailable for a wallet (most often CLV, due to gaps in Polymarket's own price-history data), its weight is redistributed across the remaining components rather than penalizing the wallet. This is a heuristic ranking signal, not investment advice — past performance does not guarantee future results, and copying a trade at a different price than the original carries different risk/reward.
